Campaign Marketing - ICBC(Rednote Post)

← Our Work Info

Overview

To promote the "Yueai Series Credit Card" (meaning "Enjoy Yourself, Love Life") specifically designed for female clients by the Industrial and Commercial Bank of China, I led the creation of four differentiated inspirational posters for women. This series accurately depicts the core images of different career stages (new professionals, elite women, female university students, and homemakers) and combines them with a customized panda mascot to vividly present the exclusive benefits of the credit card for women.

Who I AM

LOUIE NG

Creative Designer & Brand Storyteller

I'm Louie (NG Yu Him), a multidisciplinary designer driven by curiosity about how design shapes human connections. My journey began at HKDI (Hong Kong Design Institution) where I earned my Higher Diploma in Advertising Design, distinguishing myself through brand collaborations with Tamjai International and Xiaomi.

What fuels my design practice is a fundamental belief: every visual conversation should reveal unexpected perspectives. Through my studio Discover Infinite Laboratory, I approach each project as an experiment - using design as a prism to refract the multifaceted relationships between brands and their audiences. Advertising, to me, isn't just persuasion; it's about creating dimensional dialogues where consumer needs and brand identities intersect in surprising ways.

Upcycling and Innovation

In Hong Kong's fiercely competitive F&B industry, TamJai International chose an innovative approach to address environmental concerns—transforming kitchen waste, specifically surplus pork belly, into personal care products through technological processes. This groundbreaking circular economy model, however, faced a communication challenge: how to help consumers understand and appreciate the environmental value behind this technology? This became the key problem our design team needed to solve. We decided to create a visual symbol that could embody this eco-conscious philosophy—the "Pork Angel."

Upcycling and Innovation

This mascot blends cha chaan teng motifs with food waste upcycling superpowers, replacing stern environmental messaging with an endearing piglet persona. Its design balances charm and purpose: the pink avatar fosters emotional bonds while demonstrating TamJai's sustainability science. Beyond visuals, we built an IP ecosystem spanning 3D animations, eco-conscious merchandise, and shareable social content. This omnichannel approach converted environmental advocacy into viral-friendly experiences consumers willingly embraced.

See beyond, create beyond.

– LOUIE NG
